About Developer

My name is カソラ (Kasora), a fursuiter and Skeb commissioner. I am the solo developer for Skeb Sniper, a private commercial software, as well as the owner, developer, and manager of kasorashibainu.com.

Since I live in the United States, I often missed the opportunity to commission artists on Skeb because of the time difference. In my case, I had been waiting for over two years to commission やふくろ (yafukuro), only to realize that their slots usually opened in the afternoon in Japan, which is early morning for me. By the time I was awake, their commissions were already closed. Since they only open two to three times a year, I never really had a chance to commission them, and I just couldn't compete with commissioners in the same time zone as the artist. I also learned that a friend of mine woke up at 3 AM just to send a request to their favorite artists because their slots always fill up in a couple of minutes, so I created Skeb Sniper to solve this problem for myself and my friends who also struggle to secure a slot. Now with Skeb Sniper, whether we're sleeping, in a meeting, or traveling, we can always send a request to our favorite artists without disrupting whatever we're doing.

Finally, I would like to thank the amazing artists who made the drawings for me on this website. The icon and background were illustrated by とおぼえふうすけ (tooboefuusuke), and the portrait on the right was created by がみ (gami).
