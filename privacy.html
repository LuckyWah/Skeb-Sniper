Google Data We Access

The Application requires access to Google user data through the Gmail API. Specifically, we request the following scope:

  • Gmail Read-Only Access: This allows us to retrieve the contents of emails after detecting new emails via Pub/Sub notifications. This scope does not permit modification, deletion, or sending of emails.

We do not use any other Google API scopes.

How We Use Your Data

The Application processes your Gmail data strictly for the following purposes:

  • Detecting new emails by monitoring Gmail metadata via Pub/Sub notifications.
  • Retrieving new emails upon receiving a Pub/Sub notification.
  • Filtering out irrelevant emails by checking if the sender is in the predefined list. If not, the email is ignored.
  • Extracting the subject and body only from relevant emails.
  • Analyzing the email subject and body to interpret its content and categorize it appropriately.

We do not modify, delete, or send emails.

Data Retention and Deletion

Our application does not retain any data, including information about your Gmail account or email contents.

All Gmail data is processed in real-time and is never stored or transferred. Once an email is retrieved and processed, no user data is retained.

If you wish to stop using the application, you can revoke access at any time via your Google Account settings. Revoking access will immediately prevent our application from accessing your Gmail data.

Gmail Token Protection and Security

We prioritize the security of your Gmail account by implementing strict protective measures:

  • All API requests comply with Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication protocol.
  • We use multi-layer encryption to encrypt your Gmail OAuth token stored on your machine.
  • We do not store or transfer your Gmail token.
