Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/09/2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the use of your information when you use the application. It also explains your privacy and how we protect your data. By using the application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Words with capitalized initials have meanings defined under the following conditions. These definitions apply whether the words appear in singular or plural.

Definitions

The definitions are as follows:

  • Application: Refers to Skeb Sniper, the software provided by the developer.
  • We: Also referred to as "we," "us," or "our," refers to the developer.
  • Data: Any information that relates to a user.
  • You: The individual accessing or using the application.

Collection of Payment Information

We collect the email address used for payment as proof of transaction and store it in our database for license activation and validation. This is the only information we collect from you.

Use of Email Accounts

Gmail Access Permissions

The application requires access to Gmail user data through the Gmail API using the Read-Only scope. We access:

  • History ID: To process only new emails received after the application starts.
  • Sender: To filter emails based on a predefined list of allowed senders.
  • Body: To retrieve and interpret the body of relevant emails.

Outlook Access Permissions

The application requires access to Outlook user data through the Microsoft Graph API. We request the following permissions:

  • Mail.Read: To read your email messages.
  • User.Read: To sign in and read your basic profile information, such as your email address.

How the Application Uses Your Email Data

The application accesses and processes your Gmail and Outlook data in real-time for the following purposes:

  • Detecting new emails via Pub/Sub notifications.
  • Retrieving and filtering emails based on the sender address.
  • Extracting the subject and body of relevant emails to interpret their content.

Emails are processed immediately and discarded after interpretation. We do not modify, delete, store, or transfer your email data.

Data Retention and Deletion

The application does not retain any Gmail or Outlook data, including your email messages or account information. All data is processed in real-time and never stored. Therefore, data deletion is not applicable.

If you wish to stop using the application, you can revoke access at any time:

  • For Gmail, visit your Google Account settings.
  • For Outlook, visit your Microsoft Account settings.

Token Protection and Security

We prioritize the security of your Gmail and Outlook accounts by implementing strict protective measures:

  • All API requests comply with Google's and Microsoft's OAuth 2.0 authentication protocols.
  • Your OAuth tokens are encrypted and securely stored on your local machine.
  • We do not store or transfer your tokens to any external server.
