Ever wake up to find that your favorite artist’s commission slot has vanished? We know how frustrating it is to wait for months only to miss your chance because the slot filled up while you were asleep.

Worry no more! Skeb Sniper levels the playing field for Western users by automating the commission request process for Japanese artists who often open slots while you sleep. By acting only when Skeb sends an email notification, Skeb Sniper ensures you never miss that coveted commission!

Discover Advanced Features of Skeb Sniper

Event-Driven Commission Status Monitoring

Never visits Skeb to monitor the artist's commission status.
Only visits Skeb once to submit your request after your artist opens requests.
Instantly sends a request to your artist right after Skeb sends an email notification to your inbox.

Fully Automated Request Submission

Automated user authentication and log-in.
Automated verification with Skeb by extracting verification codes from Skeb through emails.
Embedded locking mechanism ensuring that each request is sent only once.

ToS-Compliant and Ethical

Only one request to Skeb is made per artist, which imposes less server load on Skeb than manual checks.
Only official emails from Skeb are processed.
No data is collected from Skeb.

User-Centric Designs

Distribution includes an installer for Windows and a prebuilt Docker image for Linux.
A user-friendly GUI is included in the app to handle every task.
Instructions are included in the GUI's "Help" tab.

Privacy and Security

Your data is encrypted with user-specific and machine-specific encryption mechanism to prevent any possible attacks.
Your data is only stored in your local machine.
Your data is never collected or transmitted.

Reliable Background Operation

Runs as a Windows Service on Windows and a Daemon on Linux, ensuring continuous operation after disconnecting from the server.
Automatically restarts after crashes, network issues, or system reboots.
Automatically refreshes the Gmail and Outlook token if it expires.

Ultra-Lightweight Design

No webpage is launched to monitor artists, ensuring minimal network usage.
Only processes emails from Skeb, ignoring all irrelevant emails.
Stays idle most of the time and only activates when an email from Skeb is received.
